• Sarah Tully

Sarah Tully is a web and hybrid application engineer based in Illinois. She has extensive experience developing mobile and web applications using Angular, RxJS, Ionic, Cordova, and Electron.

Sarah is an advocate for Human Rights and the LGBT community.

Work

For the last 4 years Sarah has been developing applications at Lextech Mobile. As technical lead, she and her team develop hybrid mobile applications and web applications for a broad range of highly respected clients including: CDW, DSC Logistics, Anheuser-Busch, Intrawest, AstraZeneca, and MetLife.
